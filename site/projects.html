Space Travel Simulation

Explore on Observable →

This interactive visualization explores principles of astrodynamics and orbital mechanics. Developed on Observable, it allows users to simulate interplanetary missions and visualize trajectories in 3D space. This project merges physics, creative coding, and educational design to make complex spaceflight scenarios intuitive.

During my time with the Seattle Flu Study, I supported development and maintenance of interactive dashboards to track COVID-19 and influenza trends across Washington. These dashboards informed stakeholders and the public through clean visualizations of high-frequency, real-time clinical data.
